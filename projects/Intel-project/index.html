03 — Design North Star

Six Principles for Luminous Support

Not arbitrary heuristics — philosophical commitments. Each addressed a specific failure pattern in Intel's existing ecosystem.

Proactive Prescience

Auto-detect hardware. Anticipate failures for that specific SKU. Surface solutions before the user articulates the problem. Support before the search.

Empathetic Authority

Warm yet technically precise. Acknowledge frustration before solutions. "I understand thermal throttling is alarming — let's decode what's happening together."

Seamless Symbiosis

Text + screenshot upload + log analysis + quick-action constellations. The interface adapts to how each user naturally communicates. Human-machine harmony.

Trustworthy Transparency

Show confidence levels. Admit limitations with grace. One-tap human escalation preserving full conversational memory. AI that earns trust through humility.

Delightful Momentum

Micro-animations, progress auras, victory confetti on resolution. Support shouldn't feel clinical — every fix should feel like a small triumph.

Inclusive Precision

WCAG 2.1 AA native. Jargon-free mode for novices. Screen-reader optimized. Voice-ready architecture for Intel AI PC NPU integration.

06 — Impact & Vision

Transformative Potential

Key Learnings

  • AI earns trust through humility. Showing confidence levels ("I'm 92% sure this is the right driver") built more trust than absolute certainties.
  • Context preservation is the killer feature. Escalation memory — carrying full conversation to human agents — was the most praised capability.
  • Personality is product, not polish. The warm, witty voice was the primary driver of trust, engagement, and NPS uplift.

The Bigger Vision

  • Voice on AI PCs — NPU-powered, privacy-first voice companion running locally on Intel Core Ultra
  • Intel DSA Fusion — Native integration with Intel Driver & Support Assistant for auto-fix workflows
  • Agentic Autonomy — Schedules repairs, orders parts, and follows up — without human prompting
  • AI Everywhere Emblem — From silicon manufacturer to humanity's silicon ally
