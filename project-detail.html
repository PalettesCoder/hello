Context

The Reality of Enterprise Complexity.

The platform served large organizations managing hundreds of concurrent initiatives across multiple departments, regions, and timelines. Operational visibility for Portfolio performance, execution, risk exposure, and financial health was functionally complete.

User Sentiment "From a functional perspective, nothing was missing. From a human perspective, everything was overwhelming."
The Comprehension Gap "I still have to call my PM to understand what's actually happening despite having 150 project views."

The system optimized for completeness. But humans operate on comprehension. This project was a redesign that would move beyond interface improvement into something fundamentally different — designing software that helps organizations understand themselves.

The Core Paradox "We have more data than ever, yet we understand less than before."

AI as an Interpreter, Not an Automation Tool.

Introducing AI created a new challenge: Trust. Rather than positioning AI as automation, I positioned it as Translation.

Interpretive Governance

Governance & Trust

Designing Trust Through Consistency.

Enterprise success depends on predictability. We implemented systemic consistency—unified color semantics, shared interaction patterns, and persistent contextual breadcrumbs. Consistency reduced interpretation effort and increased organizational confidence.
