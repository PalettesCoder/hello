User Personas

A detailed look at the primary end-users of the application, analyzing their fundamental needs, technical constraints, and strategic goals.

👩🏻‍💼

System Manager

Age: 34 • Tech-Savvy

Needs

  • Quick overview of vital statistics.
  • Intuitive data visualization templates.
  • Easily track department workflows.

Frustrations

  • Too much clutter on the dashboard.
  • Slow loading times for complex reports.
👨🏼‍💻

Data Analyst

Age: 28 • Highly Analytical

Needs

  • Deep dive capabilities into secondary metrics.
  • Quick export options for CSV & PDF.
  • Advanced filtering mechanisms.

Frustrations

  • Hidden tools behind nested menus.
  • Lack of keyboard shortcuts.
